Mallory McCorkhill: Electronic Portfolio

Welcome to My E-Portfolio


Purpose

As part of my culminating dual-degree experience, I have published this E-Portfolio as a repository showcasing my work over the course of my Masters program. Each project demonstrates core competencies falling under one of the required categories.

The e-portfolio is meant to be a product that collects and displays my coursework and professional activities. As such, I have chosen to make it a project in itself demonstrating what I have learned in the Digital Technologies course series, which I completed at the end of the Fall 2019 semester. Additionally, I have employed knowledge I gained during my graduate assistantship to host the site on GitHub, so that it can persist beyond my graduation from Kent State University.

Description

This website contains sections for various components of my program of study. These include my required Portfolio Projects, completed throughout my program during various required and elective courses, as well as the required Practicum Experiences completed during my on-site hours at Stow-Munroe Falls High School over the course of the Fall 2019 semester as part of my culminating experience. Additionally, my updated Resumé is available, detailing my professional and extracurricular activities as a graduate assistant for the iSchool. The two Values Statements/Reflections completed at different stages of my program are available. Finally, I have included Additional Links that do not fall into a specific category from my Program of Study. These include other work I have completed and resources that are pertinent to my education and career goals.
